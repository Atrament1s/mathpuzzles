>$$ \\ $$
>
>$$
>\text{Convert } 0.235 \text{ to a percentage.}
>$$
>
>$$ \\ $$

$$ \\ $$
To convert $0.235$ to a percentage, we can multiply it by $100$:
$$ \\ $$

$$
\begin{gather}
&\quad&\quad 0.235 \times 100 \\ \\
&=&\quad 2.35 \times 10 \\ \\
&=&\quad 23.5 \times 1 \\ \\
&=&\quad 23.5 \\ \\
&=&\quad \enclose{circle}{\ 23.5\% \ }
\end{gather}
$$

$$ \\ $$

Therefore, $0.235$ converted to a percentage is $23.5\%$.
